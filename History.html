History

From then until now!

2013

In 2013, Manila's Cookie started with the goal to make and serve the freshest and most delicious cookies to satistify every customers cookie cravings. The brand believed that since cookies are the perfect portion size and they’re so easy to take on the go, it is the best and perfect dessert for people to buy and share. According to them, unlike other desserts, cookies do not require additional utensils or tableware. Simply pick up and enjoy. The classic chocolate chip was the first product that was made and sold.

2015

The recipe starts with a dough composed of flour, butter, both brown and white sugar, semi-sweet chocolate chips, eggs, and vanilla. They tried and tested formula of delivering great-tasting cookie until they successfully got the right and exact flavor. That was the time their brand was born. In 2015, the first Manila's Cookie franchise opened. Since then, the brand has grown to more than 50+ shop in the Philippines.

2020

In 2017, Manila's Cookie continued to developed and refined their own versions, using local ingredients and methods, and making them to fit our own tastes and to have a true impact on every cookie that is served to every costumer. In addition, Manila's Cookie offers a new lineup of soft chewy cookies, including oreo cheesecake, matcha, almond, peanut butter and more delicious baked goods. Manila's Cookie Today (2020) Manila's Cookie is constantly innovating to offer people new, delicious flavors and menu options to keep them running all throughout the day.

Manila's Cookie

Manila's Cookie started in 2013, owned by Riz Allyson Aranas. "In my lifetime, I have faced many obstacles, the largest of which pushed me to the edge so hard that I felt I would never get up again. Oddly enough, it was through that obstacle that I discovered my true calling and created a small cookie business that was successful beyond what I had ever thought possible. We also decided to build this business because cookies hold a lot of nostalgia for people."
